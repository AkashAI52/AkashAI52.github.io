Miscellaneous

    • An Introduction to Multivariable Mathematics - Leon Simon, Stanford University
      A comprehensive set of lecture notes covering multivariable calculus, linear algebra, and optimization. The notes are well-structured and provide a rigorous treatment of the mathematical foundations necessary for advanced studies in optimization and Machine Learning.
    • Matrix Computations - Gene H. Golub and Charles F. Van Loan
      A gold-standard reference for numerical linear algebra. Highly recommended for computational researchers working with large-scale matrix problems.
    • Topology - James R. Munkres
      A classic and rigorous introduction to topology. Excellent for building strong foundations in open/closed sets, continuity, and compactness.
    • Convex and Discrete Geometry - Peter M. Gruber
      A comprehensive text on convex geometry, covering topics such as convex bodies, polytopes, and geometric inequalities. Useful for researchers interested in the geometric aspects of optimization.
    • Differential equations and the calculus of variations - L. ELSGOLTS
      A classic and rigorous introduction to differential equations and the calculus of variations. Excellent for building strong foundations in these areas of mathematics.
    • Naive Set Theory - Paul R. Halmos
      A classic and rigorous introduction to set theory. Excellent for building strong foundations in mathematical logic and foundations.
